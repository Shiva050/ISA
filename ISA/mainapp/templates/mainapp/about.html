About Us

The Indian Student Association is an independent, non-profit student organization recognized by the University of Houston - Clear Lake.

A student organization that is a family to the Indian students studying away from home, we strive hard to keep India and its cultural heritage alive in our hearts. We welcome your participation and involvement to help us grow into a community that everyone would like to be a part of.
