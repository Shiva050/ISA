Membership

Want to join hands with ISA?
ISA accepts members throughout the year.

Membership Fee: $10
Validity: One academic year

Benefits of Membership

  • Eligible for DS Labs Scholarship: DS labs is a scholarship exclusively for the members of ISA. The scholarship award is open every fall and Spring Semesters. The award amount depends on the funds available with the financial aid office.
  • Discounted entry fees for all ISA events: Members are eligible for additional discounts for all events organized by ISA and also at the bake sales.
  • Free entry for Guest lectures and many more
